# Project ideas

## shoplocal

**Concept:** A curated feed of unstructured offers available closest to you.

- Proximity-first: "buy cappuccino 40m away", "discount Christmas decorations 100m away", etc.
- Unstructured offers (not a structured product catalog) — real-world deals, promos, one-offs.
- Sorted/filtered by distance so the nearest relevant offers surface first.

## babblinggpt

**Concept:** A tiny audio language model in the browser that babbles like a baby (random audio tokens) and learns from you via in-browser backprop when you talk back to it.

- Runs entirely in the browser (no server for inference/training).
- Generates “babbling” — random or weakly structured audio tokens strung together, baby-like.
- You talk back to it; the model does backprop in the browser based on that interaction (e.g. reward / loss from your response or alignment with your speech).
- Goal: minimal, playful “audio LM that learns from conversation” demo.
