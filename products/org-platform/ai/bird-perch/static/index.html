Bird Perch

Continuous listening streams your mic over a WebSocket; the server keeps a rolling buffer and re-runs the classifier so the best match updates while you listen. You can also record a short clip or upload audio. The backend runs Google’s bird-vocalization-classifier (TensorFlow 2 on Kaggle) and returns the nearest species matches in logit space (softmax-ranked).

Continuous listening

Uses your microphone — allow access when prompted. Audio is sent as short WebM chunks; the server decodes into a ring buffer and periodically re-runs the classifier so you see a current best species and top five while audio keeps flowing. Heavier than a single upload — on a small VPS, use a sensible infer interval (see BIRDPERCH_STREAM_INFER_SEC).
