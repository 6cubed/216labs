Bird Perch

Record a short clip of a bird call or upload audio. The backend runs Google’s bird-vocalization-classifier (TensorFlow 2 on Kaggle) and returns the nearest species matches in logit space (softmax-ranked).

Live listen

Streams short WebM chunks over a WebSocket; the server keeps a rolling buffer and periodically re-runs the classifier so you see a current best species while you listen. Heavier than a single upload — use a sensible infer interval on small VPS.
